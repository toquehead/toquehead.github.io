Travel to Shark Bay

It took you how long?

Shark Bay is on the west coast of Western Australia:

 

That is a long way from here. We got from here to there by:

  1. Driving for 8 hours to Vancouver.
  2. Taking a 2.5 hour flight from Vancouver to LA.
  3. Waiting 2 hours in LA for our next flight.
  4. Flying 12 hours to Auckland (non-stop).
  5. Waiting 7.5 hours in Auckland for our next flight (it was late).
  6. Flying for 7 hours to Perth.
  7. Taking the wonderful Greyhound for another 11 hours to Overlander Roadhouse ("Roadhouse" is Australian for a gas station in the middle of nowhere).
  8. Waiting a few hours at the Overlander for our next bus.
  9. And finally, riding a bus the last couple hours to Denham.

For a total of 54 hours.

Admittedly, we did spend a couple nights in Vancouver at my folks place, and three nights in Perth visiing friends, but it was still fairly grueling, especially when you consider we were travelling with a kayak and gear totaling 100 kg.
