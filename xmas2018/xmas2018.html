Seasons Greetings from Doug and Sandy

Another trip around our star, and another Christmas letter from Doug and Sandy.

For us, it was a year of the usual stuff, and some not so usual stuff. After taking a break from house hunting in January, we returned to the NSW South Coast to resume the search for a place to call home, which meant the usual heading out with the tribe in the kayaks exploring caves ...

... and our beautiful coast.

The house hunting adventure was a long drawn out and excruciatingly painful affair. By the time Antipidean autumn came around, we'd just about had enough, so ventured off for some adventures a bit further afield. We had long aspired to paddle around the stunning Wilsons Promitory, the rugged granite cape that is the southern most point of mainland Australia, and is infamous for its fearsome weather. In March, with three other members of the tribe, we had a grand adventure around The Prom.

Later in the fall, a weather window opened up for the mystical Nadgee , a wild and beautiful coast reveered by ocean kayakers. We nipped off with our good mate Snoady for a magnificent trip in ideal conditions: NSW's best multi-day ocean kayak trip.

And then, after 8 months of real estate shopping, just when we'd pretty much given up, we found a house that ticked off enough of the boxes that we bought it a few days later. It's a lovely little brick and siding house, with high ceilings to take in the sunny exposure and our small but productive garden. We can trolley our kayaks down to the beach in 5 minutes and ride our mountain bikes to the local single track in 20 minutes. We are as close to NSW's best rock climbing as we can get without being too near the sprawling stressed-out metropolis known as Sydney. Our house backs onto a reserve with towering spotted gums that is stuffed with bower birds, blue tongued lizards, and all manner of parrots. We are truly blessed with a wonderful group of characters in our tribe and are enthralled with our new place in life.

And then we did something really crazy. We went skiing. In Australia. And it was good! We're not talking blower pow here, but we had a great time rolling through the high country on a solid 2 m pack. The perfect corn snow made for sweet descents ... if only they were a bit longer.

We wish you a frightfully pleasant holiday season and a super fantastic 2019.
